How can a QC Engineer be useful?

QC Engineer is a specialist who has certain knowledge and skills that help him to systematically and systematically look for problems in the product. QA knows how to find errors, analyzes the conditions for their occurrence, systematizes and sends a test report to the development department, according to which programmers can make changes to their code as quickly and efficiently as possible.
